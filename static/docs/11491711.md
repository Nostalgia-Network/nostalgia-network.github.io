## Rules

**No Racism or Hate Speech**
Any form of racism, slurs, or hate speech is strictly prohibited. This includes offensive language, imagery, or content aimed at any individual or group based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexuality, or any other characteristic.

**Avoid Sensitive Topics**
Please refrain from discussing or sharing memes about politics, religion, or other sensitive topics. These conversations often lead to unnecessary conflicts and are not appropriate for this server.

**Enjoy Yourself**
Have fun and enjoy your time here! Engage positively with others and make the most out of your experience.

**Consequences for Rule Violations**
These rules apply to both this Discord server and our Unturned server(s). Violating any of these rules may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban, depending on the severity of the offense. Moderators will handle situations on a case-by-case basis.

## Support

To keep our community enjoyable for everyone, we utilize a centralized support system through our Discord Server. To ensure your request is seen, please create a post under #issues in the Discord.

If submitting a player report, please make sure to include the following information (reports without evidence will be discarded):
- Player name
- Screenshot/video evidence
- Time/date of the incident (including your time zone)

Our staff are here to help, but they must also follow the rules. If you have a complaint regarding a specific staff member's behavior, please send a direct message on Discord to the server owner, rather than opening a standard ticket.
